Selected work

Baroque Futures

Reimagining touch

A modular, tactile bionic arm built with photonic sensors and sustainable PLA casting. I lead research, motion modeling, and user testing to ensure every interaction feels expressive and humane.

Roles include biomechanical CAD, sensor calibration, and interface prototyping that bridges hardware with inclusive UI systems for future myoelectric control.
